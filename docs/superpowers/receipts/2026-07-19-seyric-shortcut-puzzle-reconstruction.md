# Seyric shortcut puzzle reconstruction receipt

## Result and review standard

Production validation now applies `findEmptyCutShortcutHosts` to every Seyric start after canonical duplicate validation. Detected hosts are rejected unless the puzzle is one of the three intentional policy exceptions: `forked-veil`, `echoed-veil`, or `atomic-fragment-erasure`.

The experiential review below evaluates the decisions offered by the final starts and their real-kernel causal evidence. It does not require an authored route to be the only or shortest solution. For a creative route to count, its new content or boundary must make a concrete operation on pre-existing content legal when that operation was illegal before; nearby, partial, larger, wrong-host, or wrong-boundary choices must not create that same local affordance. Renamed owners, additional operands, larger syntax, and layout changes do not by themselves establish a different decision.

Post-repair independent verdicts for Tasks 3, 5, and 6 are recorded with reviewer identities, reviewed ranges, explicit verdicts, finding severity, and concrete evidence below. Every affected shortcut-review cell references its exact verdict record.
